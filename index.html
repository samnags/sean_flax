Sean Flax

Award-Winning Producer / Entrepreneur

About

Sean is an award-winning producer and entrepreneur. He has worked for multinational publishers including the BBC, NPR, Eurovision, NHK, and Time Warner Cable (Spectrum) - where his content was seen by millions of viewers. Since leaving journalism, Sean has co-founded two media startups, and his independent work has appeared on NBC Sports Network, CBS Sunday Morning, USA Today, New York Magazine, Sports Illustrated and many other broadcast and digital platforms.


Sean is based in New York City, where he was born, and graduated from the University of Michigan.
